I'm very into cycling. It is one of my main hobbies and is my main form of exercise. I ride to get to work and to go shopping, and I ride on and off-road for fun when I can find the time. In the past I have done alot of riding at night, and have had some fun putting together lighting systems for doing so. Nowadays most of the time I ride I have my kids with me, either on their own bikes or on the back of tandems piloted by myself or my husband. Cycling always has been a part of my life, and (as long as I am physically capable) it will continue to be.
